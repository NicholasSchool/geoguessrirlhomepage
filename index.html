How To Play:

Upon clicking the button below You will be given a random picture of a location near you. Your task will then be to go out into the world and take a picture of the space around you, trying to match that exact picture. You will then be able to compare how close you are, and you can keep trying to get it as close as possible! When you are ready with your image the AI will give you a score, and tell you if you've gotten it correct! To play with friends, try to be the first person to match one or more images!
